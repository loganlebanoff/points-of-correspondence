`` I 'm all for photos in museums as that is a memory -- a personal memory -- of your visit , '' Dixon wrote in an email .

But not selfie sticks , which she says allow you to invade other people 's personal space .

`` They 're just as bad IMO as tripods or opening an umbrella .

`` There is also a risk , while lining up your shot with a selfie stuck inside a museum or gallery , of knocking other people around you or worse , the art , '' Dixon wrote .
