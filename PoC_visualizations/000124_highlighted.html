The 21-year-old golfer equaled Tiger Woods ' 1997 record winning score of 18 under par to realize his childhood ambitions of donning the green jacket on Sunday as he swept through the field to become Masters champion at Augusta .

An ice-cool display made him the second-youngest winner -- behind only Woods , who was 155 days younger that year -- and only the fifth man to have led from start to finish over the tournament 's four days .
