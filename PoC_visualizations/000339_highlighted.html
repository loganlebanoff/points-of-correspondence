( CNN ) An Alaska Airlines flight was forced to make an emergency landing Monday after its pilot reported hearing unusual banging .

Flight 448 , which had been bound for Los Angeles , returned to Seattle .

Upon landing , a ramp agent was discovered inside the front cargo hold , according to a statement from the airline .

The agent , who is an employee of Alaska contractor Menzies Aviation , told authorities he had fallen asleep , the statement said .

He appeared to be in OK condition .

The cargo hold is pressurized and temperature controlled .

The plane was also only in the air for 14 minutes , Alaska Airlines said .

CNN affiliate KOMO spoke to Marty Collins , a passenger on the plane .

`` When we landed was when all the trucks and the police and the fire trucks surrounded the plane . ''

The agent was taken to an area hospital as a precaution .

All ramp employees have security badges , and undergo full criminal background checks before being hired , said Alaska Airlines .

A total of 170 passengers and six crew members were on Flight 448 .
