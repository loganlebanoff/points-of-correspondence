( CNN ) Kerry Washington is headed for HBO .

The `` Scandal '' star has signed on to play Anita Hill and executive produce a TV movie for the premium cable network , The Hollywood Reporter has learned .

Called `` Confirmation , '' the telepic -- which is in development from HBO Films -- will detail the explosive 1991 Clarence Thomas-Hill Supreme Court nomination hearings , which brought the country to a standstill and forever changed the way people think about sexual harassment , victims ' rights and modern-day race relations .
